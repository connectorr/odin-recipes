Egg Fried Rice

Go back to HOME

Description 📖

Egg fried rice is a classic comfort food that's incredibly simple to make. Fluffy scrambled eggs are tossed with cold rice and colorful vegetables in a hot wok, all coated in a savory soy sauce seasoning. It's the perfect way to use leftover rice and turns a few humble ingredients into a delicious, satisfying meal in just 10 minutes. It's quick, budget-friendly, and guaranteed to make everyone smile.Enjoy! 🍛

Ingredients 🛒

For the Rice

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, day-old)
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn - fresh or frozen)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish

  • Spring onions, chopped

Step By Step Process

Step 1: Prep Everything

  1. If using fresh vegetables, dice the carrots and onion finely.
  2. Beat the eggs in a small bowl with a pinch of salt.
  3. Have all ingredients ready near the stove—this dish cooks very fast!

Step 2: Scramble the Eggs

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large wok or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Pour in the beaten eggs and let them set for 30 seconds.
  3. Gently scramble them with a spatula until just cooked but still soft.
  4. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.

Step 3: Sauté Aromatics and Veggies

  1. Add the remaining 1 tbsp oil to the same pan.
  2. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender.

Step 4: Add the Rice

  1. Add the cold cooked rice to the pan. Break up any clumps with your spatula.
  2. Stir and toss for 2-3 minutes until the rice is heated through and well mixed.

Step 5: Season and Combine

  1. Add soy sauce, black pepper, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  2. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan.
  3. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes until well combined and heated through.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

  1. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  2. Garnish generously with chopped spring onions.
  3. Serve hot as a meal or side dish.
